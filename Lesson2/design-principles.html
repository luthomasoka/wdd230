Proximity

Microsoft

microsoft.com

Proximity is used by designers to illustrate relationship between a number of elements (whether they are connected or not). This Microsoft webpage shows exactly that. The Microsoft products are grouped closely together from other elements of the page.

Allignment

Apple

apple.com

There is commonly two types of allignment; vertical or horizontal allignment. vertical allignment is when the top, middle or bottom items on a page allign on a horizontal line. Horizontal allignment is the allignment of right, middle or left sides of items on a vertical line on a page. This image from the Apple store shows a horizontal allignment of images.

Repetition

Amazon

amazon.com

Repetition may be used through patterns to incorporate unity and familiarity to a design or layout as shown on the above image of the Amazon store.
