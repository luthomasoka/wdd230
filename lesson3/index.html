Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform the general public of the Nelson Mandela Bay, of the existance and objectives of the Business Chamber. Tho other key purpose of the site is to invite established businesses in and around the Bay to join our efforts in strengthening the economy of our city and foster the business to business networking. The pictures should showcase various company logos that are part of the chamber. The pictures should be appealing and be reminiscent of the type of services that the chamber offers.The theme should be very professional. The visitor should clearly be aware of the businesses involved and the chamber's objective.
